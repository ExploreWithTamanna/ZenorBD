FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

What is EgPay?

EgPay is a powerful yet easy to utilize digital solution created to streamline business exchanges. Leveraging individual accounts as gateways, it guarantees adaptability, protection, and simplicity of use. With EgPay, enterprises can effortlessly approve installments specifically through their sites, improving client benefit.

Is EgPay a Safe Option for Businesses?

Yes, EgPay prioritizes security with end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and automatic fraud detection. Each transaction is verified and secured to ensure client trust and data integrity. Our platform meets industry-standard security protocols.

What payment methods are supported?

EgPay supports 12 different payment methods including Mobile Banking, Bank Transfers, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, and various digital wallets. This ensures your customers can pay using their preferred method.

How does the integration process work?

Integration is seamless with our robust API. Our flexible design interfaces with a wide range of platforms to automate workflows and reconcile funds with minimal human intervention. We provide comprehensive documentation and 24/7 support.

Are there any transaction fees?

No, EgPay offers unlimited transactions without any fees. This makes it cost-effective for businesses of all sizes, from small startups to large organizations.
